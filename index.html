Research

My current work studies high-performance blockchain execution, authenticated state commitments, and trusted relay infrastructure.

Blockchain Execution Acceleration

I optimize blockchain execution across scheduling, execution engines, and data layers, including deterministic parallel scheduling, reusable AOT compilation, and height-optimized authenticated data structures.

Trusted Relay Infrastructure

I study TEE-based relay security mechanisms that minimize the trusted computing base and protect forwarding logic against leakage, prompt injection, and response tampering.
